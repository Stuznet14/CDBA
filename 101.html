Charlotte Dragon Boat Association
Join Us on the Water! Embrace the Spirit of Love, Pride, and Unity in Dragon Boating

At the Charlotte Dragon Boat Association, we are more than just a team we're a community bound by passion and camaraderie. As a non-profit organization, our mission is to promote the thrilling and dynamic sport of dragon boating throughout our region. Whether you're a seasoned paddler or new to the sport, we welcome you to experience the excitement, build lasting friendships, and take pride in being part of something special. Join us and paddle your way to new adventures!
